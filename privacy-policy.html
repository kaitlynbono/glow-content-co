Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

This privacy policy describes how Glow Content Co handles data in connection with our Pinterest automation tool, which automatically posts pins to Pinterest on behalf of our business account to promote our Etsy digital products.

Data We Access

  • Pinterest account profile information (username, account ID)
  • Pinterest boards (names and IDs) associated with our account
  • Pins created by this application

How We Use Data

Data is used solely to create and publish pins linking to our Etsy digital product listings, read our boards to post to the correct board, and track posting activity to avoid duplicates.

Data Storage

We do not store Pinterest user data on external servers. Pin metadata is stored in a private internal database only to track which products have been pinned. No personal data of Pinterest users is collected or stored.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose any data to third parties. All API access is limited to our own Pinterest business account.

Third-Party Services

This tool uses the Pinterest API (v5). Use is subject to Pinterest's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy at https://policy.pinterest.com/en/privacy-policy

Contact

For questions, contact us at: glowcontentco@gmail.com

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The last updated date at the top reflects any changes.
